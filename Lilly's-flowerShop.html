Gallery page

During my first year of studies in Applied Computer Science at Thomas More, I gained valuable insights into web design. As part of the advanced web design course, we collaborated on a group project to develop a website for a flower shop. I particularly enjoyed working on this project, and one of the pages I contributed to was the gallery page of the webshop. This page allows users to browse through all the flowers, and upon hovering over a flower, additional explanations about it are displayed.

This is the link of the final website we created as a group: Lillys-flowershop

About page

In addition to the gallery page, I also made the "About Me" page for the website. This page features additional information about Lilly, the owner of the shop. To enhance its visual appeal, I incorporated a Bootstrap card. Furthermore, I integrated the brand colors we selected for the site, predominantly featuring floral hues such as pink and purple.
