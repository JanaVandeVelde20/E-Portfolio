MJ entertainment homepage

During my free time, I created a website dedicated to providing entertainment for kids' parties and various events. The offerings on this platform include both shows and workshops, featuring activities such as balloon twisting, circus performances, and magic tricks.

The development process involved the use of Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, enhancing the site's visual appeal and functionality.
If you're curious to explore the result of my efforts, click on the following link: MJ Entertainment

Reflection:
By creating this website it deepened my understanding of webdesign, particularly the use of Bootstrap.

Gallery

On my website, I've also made a Gallery page showcasing various facets of my work: street performances, balloon twisting at parties, captivating moments from performing in a traveling circus, and more. The layout I've opted for is quite simplistic: images arranged in circles with titles accompanying them. Rather than cluttering the page with text, I've let the images take center stage, allowing them to speak for themselves.
