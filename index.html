My Portfolio

Hello! I'm Jana Vande Velde, and I'm thrilled to welcome you to my portfolio. Here, you'll find a collection of projects I've been passionately working on during my journey as an applied computer science student at Thomas More.

Website MJ entertainment

During my free time, I created a website dedicated to providing entertainment for kids' parties and various events. The offerings on this platform include both shows and workshops, featuring activities such as balloon twisting, circus performances, and magic tricks. The development process involved the use of Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, enhancing the site's visual appeal and functionality.

Data Visualisation Project

For the Data Visualization course project, we focused on wildlife strikes in aviation, aiming to inform people using various types of graphs, buttons and images. The project consists of four pages, each addressing specific questions.
I found this project very interesting to work on. I gained more insight on how to visualise data by using different kind of graphs. Moreover, it taught me the art of storytelling through the interpretation of data and graphs.

Recipe Organiser App

 For DevOps & Security we got to choose our own project. I choose to design a recipe organizer using C# and Windows Forms. The application utilizes SQLite for local recipe storage, and I implemented the CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) principles to manage recipe data. Besides that, I used the ORM tool Dapper to facilitate database interactions.
